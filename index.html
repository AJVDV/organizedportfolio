About Me

What brought me to this field

Like many from my generation, and especially those after, a huge amount of my time is spent within the realms of digital interaction and consumption. It only makes sense that after having so much interest in web interactions, video games, and the digital age as a whole that I would become drawn to software engineering and coding as a career. Finding that I have some natural talent, a good thought process for debugging, and creatively designing functional code, I have thus far thrived within the space. My passion for this creative process has only grown in the time I've spent with it, and is immensly rewarding for me.

Where I am based

Originally I am from Scottsbluff, a small town in Western Nebraska, but I moved to Washington in 2019. I am located in Kent Washington. This is very near to Seattle, and as such is close enough for ample networking opportunities. I am happy to live and often work near such a metro area, and have found a great amount of opportunity because of it.

Team Oriented

Despite having pride in code I have created myself, and seeing my own functional work spring into existance, I am more than excited to collaborate with others to create truly impressive projects within the digital landscape. I find that group work can be even more rewarding, and often allows for much larger projects to come to fruition. I can hardly wait to see what the future holds.

Who I am

Defining one's self is difficult, and only becomes more so when considering philosophy and existensialism, but I'd like to at least touch on who I am. In a large sense I am a work in progress, as the vast majority of us are, but that isn't to say that I feel less than or inept, I simply strive to be better than I was the day before, every day. I am a person who is quick to acknowledge my own mistakes and shortcomings, so that improvement can happen. Having a small background in counseling psychology has helped me to realize that the pursuit of bettering one's self, and the world around them by proxy is one of the best paths one can set themselves upon. I am a driven and detail oriented person. I enjoy music, video games, writing, and cooking.
